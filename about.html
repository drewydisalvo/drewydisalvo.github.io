About Drewy

DREWY DiSALVO is a Senior at Fordham University. Drewy has developed a portfolio of illustrations that embraces influences of Fauvism and abstract expressionism. Drewy primarily enjoys rendering illustration by hand and has been creating art since he was young. His photography documents travels to cities throughout Italy and Japan. Additionally, Drewy has written five feature length screenplays.
